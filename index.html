Technology that adapts to you.

About

I'm Wyeth Anzilotti, a senior at Lehigh University studying computer science and human-computer interaction. CRUMB started while watching my brother Luke navigate devices that were never built for how he experiences the world. The interfaces weren’t broken. He wasn’t broken. The way of communication was broken.

CRUMB is my response: built with Raspberry Pi Zero, NFC readers, and Python, housed in a modified calculator shell. It runs fully locally. Your data stays yours.
