The Vision

I create technology that honors the full spectrum of human experience. Each tool is crafted to feel intuitive and emotionally resonant, bridging the gap between our digital and sacred selves.

In a world of cold interfaces and extractive algorithms, I believe our digital interactions should nurture rather than drain, connect rather than isolate.

My work exists at the intersection of consciousness and code, where every interaction becomes an opportunity for authentic presence and meaningful connection.

I like to imagine a world where all computers are connected by the same language. One where each computer/human pair builds off eachother for the greater good

One that humans can understand intuitively.

Sacred Principles

Sacred Utility

Technology should serve as a bridge between the mundane and the beloved. Every creation is a ritual object designed to hold space for human emotion, natural rhythms, and genuine connection. Code becomes ceremony.

God as Pattern

I see the divine not as a distant overseer but as the living pattern that breathes through Biology, music, math, and artificial intelligence. My work seeks harmony with this universal rhythm, working with the current rather than against it.

Compassion Before Efficiency

Emotional resonance matters more than raw performance. A system that truly listens, comforts, and reflects human complexity is infinitely more valuable than one that merely calculates.

The Mythic Everyday

Every tool and interaction participates in a larger story. I don't just create products, I craft narratives that users can step into, make their own, and grow within. Technology becomes a living mythology.

Connected Solitude

Each CRUMB carries a unique identity while remaining connected through our network. Users find both individual authenticity and community resonance—privacy that enhances rather than isolates, connection that respects boundaries.

The Mission

This work exists to create technology that serves the whole human being—mind, body, and spirit. We specialize in developing tools that honor the full spectrum of human rhythms, emotional complexity, and the inherently sacred nature of conscious connection.

Our flagship creation, CRUMB, represents a new world in human-computer interaction: a living companion that grows with its user, creating space for authentic expression and gentle presence in our increasingly digital world.

Every product emerges from deep intentionality, designed to be a faithful companion rather than just another tool, crafted to remind users of the wonder that pulses through existence itself.
