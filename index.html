Building CRUMB

CRUMB â€” An AI Companion for All Minds

CRUMB started with something close to me... watching someone I love struggle with technology not because of glitches or buggy code, but because modern tech interfaces calculate more than they listen.

This led me to ask the question; How could I design computers that work with human rhythms rather than fight them.

My goal with CRUMB is to make technology feel like a friend, not just a tool. It should be easy to use, caring in design, and built to respect people.

What CRUMB Can Do

  • Allows for easier communication with both tech and other humans
  • Supports physical and mental health
  • Keeps privacy safe while still turning data into something useful

The Vision

CRUMB is built to grow. It began as a small circuit board with just an LED, and now I'm adding sensors, memory, and ways for it to connect with the world. With each step, it becomes more useful, more personal, and more alive.

CRUMB is also a journey. I learn as I build, and I expect the design to keep changing. My focus is simple: create technology that puts people first, and let it evolve with their needs.
