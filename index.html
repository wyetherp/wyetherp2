CRUMB

CRUMB began as a curiosity. A question about what computers are truly capable of. It quickly evolved into a goal of making computers not only easier for all humans to communicate with, but for computers and humans to work in harmony for the benifit of all.

A few ways that CRUMB/AI could benifit humans;

  • AI simplifying complicated workflows/supply chains
  • AI in healthcare/psichiatric worlds
  • Privacy concerns/Data simplification

How CRUMB can evolve

I see CRUMB not only as a computer, but as a new species on planet Earth. Its circuts mimick the biology of single cell organisims.

It evolves with each new update. As of now its just a circuit with a light, but once we start adding sensors, memory, communication I believe that its abilities will only grow.
