THE STORY

Where this started

I'm Wyeth Anzilotti, a senior at Lehigh University studying computer science and human-computer interaction. This project started with watching my brother Luke.

Luke's mind works differently. Watching him interact with technology made something obvious: the interfaces weren't broken. Luke wasn't broken. The match was broken.

"The problem isn't the person. The problem is the interface."

Every notification assumes you want interruption. Every UI assumes linear thinking. So I started asking: what if we built differently? CRUMB is my attempt at an answer—a companion device that adapts to you.

Built with Raspberry Pi Zero, NFC readers, Python. Housed in a calculator shell that fits in your pocket. It runs locally. Your data stays yours. No cloud dependency. No planned obsolescence.

WHY NOT JUST AN APP?

Hardware creates boundaries that software can't

Apps live in your phone. Your phone owns your attention.

CRUMB is separate. It doesn't compete with Instagram, email, or texts. It exists to help you disconnect from the attention economy—not add to it.

What makes hardware different:

  • Physical presence matters. You can hold CRUMB. Click its buttons. Feel haptic feedback. Tactile interaction creates stronger habit formation than touching glass.
  • No app store gatekeepers. Your device, your rules. Update when you want. Modify the code. Add features without waiting for approval.
  • Battery life measured in days, not hours. Low-power hardware means CRUMB doesn't die halfway through your day.
  • One function, done well. Apps get bloated with features. Hardware constraints force focus. CRUMB does companion tasks—nothing more, nothing less.
  • True privacy. No app can promise this. No permissions, no telemetry, no hidden data collection. Airgapped by design.

Apps are convenient. Hardware is intentional. CRUMB asks you to carry something extra because separation creates space for mindfulness.

TECHNICAL SPECS

What's inside Genesis

PROCESSOR

Raspberry Pi Zero W - 1GHz single-core ARM, 512MB RAM. Runs offline speech recognition, pattern learning algorithms.

CONNECTIVITY

NFC (PN532 reader/writer), Bluetooth Low Energy for optional mesh networking. No cellular. No wifi required after setup.

INTERFACE

WS2812B addressable LEDs, 4 tactile buttons, haptic motor, optional OLED display. Voice input via onboard microphone.

BATTERY

2000mAh rechargeable Li-ion. 3-5 days typical use. USB-C charging. Replaceable battery—not glued shut.

STORAGE

32GB microSD card. All data stored locally. SQLite database for routines and patterns. Fully encrypted.

SOFTWARE

Python 3.x, OpenAI Whisper (offline), custom adaptive learning algorithms. Fully open-source. MIT licensed.

WHAT YOU CAN DO

CRUMB in daily life

Morning Routine

CRUMB learns you check email at 9am. At 8:55, a gentle haptic pulse—not demanding, just reminding. No screen required.

Focus Sessions

Press a button to start a Pomodoro timer. LED changes color to show time remaining. Vibrates when it's time for a break.

Fidget-Friendly

Physical buttons you can click without looking. LED patterns you can customize. For ADHD minds that need tactile engagement.

Local Contact Sharing

Tap your CRUMB to a friend's—instant contact exchange via NFC. No app permissions, no cloud sync, no data harvesting.

Mood Tracking

Quick voice note: "Feeling anxious." CRUMB logs it locally with timestamp. Review patterns weekly without typing in apps.

Medication Reminders

Set it once, forget it. CRUMB reminds you at the same time every day. Confirm with a button press—no unlock screen needed.

PRINCIPLES

Values that guide development

01

Built in the Open

Every line of code is on GitHub. Community-driven development. Transparency builds trust. If you don't like something, fork it and make it better.

02

Yours to Keep

Your data stays on your device. No cloud uploads, no tracking. Replaceable components, repairable design. Privacy and longevity aren't features—they're the foundation.

03

Adaptive, Not Prescriptive

CRUMB learns your patterns without judging them. It adapts to you—not the other way around. No gamification, no streak anxiety, no shame.

04

Joy in Interaction

Tactile feedback matters. Small delights matter. Technology doesn't have to be cold or purely efficient to be useful. Play is part of the design.

THE ECOSYSTEM

Natural patterns of growth, connection, and life

CRUMB Genesis

"The first breath. Where the pattern begins."

A tactile companion housed in a calculator shell. Voice-responsive, pattern-learning, designed for hands that need to fidget and minds that need gentler interfaces. No planned obsolescence. No cloud dependency. Just you and a device that grows with you.

What's in the box:

• 1× CRUMB Genesis device
• 1× Tree seed to plant (oak or maple, your choice—as CRUMB learns you, something living grows alongside)
• 1× USB-C charging cable
• 1× Instruction manual (printed on recycled paper)
• 1× Welcome card explaining the philosophy
• Lifetime software updates
• Access to builder Discord community

The tree seed isn't decoration—it's ritual. Planting something that takes years to mature reminds us that growth happens slowly, intentionally. As you tend the seedling, CRUMB tends you.
