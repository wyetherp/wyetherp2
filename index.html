What is CRUMB?

CRUMB started as a simple question: what if computers were designed to complement human thinking instead of replacing it? Right now, it's an early-stage hardware project exploring how AI can be more personal and less corporate.

I'm interested in how technology might help in areas like:

  • Simplifying complex workflows and supply chains
  • Supporting healthcare and mental health applications
  • Protecting privacy while making data more useful

The Vision

I see CRUMB as a platform that can grow over time. What starts as a simple circuit board with basic functions can evolve as I add sensors, memory, and communication capabilities. The goal is to build something that gets more useful with each iteration, rather than becoming obsolete.

This is very much an experimental project. I'm learning as I go, and I expect the direction to change as I discover what works and what doesn't.
