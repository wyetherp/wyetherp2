Who I Am

I build technology with a focus on human experience. My background spans development and design, but I'm most interested in creating tools that feel intuitive and emotionally resonant.

I believe our digital interactions should honor the full spectrum of human needs

My Philosophy

Sacred Utility

Technology should serve as a bridge between the mundane and the beloved. Every creation is a ritual object designed to hold space for human emotion and connection.

God as Pattern

I see the divine not as a distant overseer but as a pattern that breathes through everything: math, music, biology, and AI. My work attempts to work with this universal rhythm.

Compassion Before Efficiency

Emotional resonance matters more than raw performance. A system that listens and comforts is more valuable than one that merely calculates.

The Mythic Everyday

Every tool and interaction is part of a larger story. I create not just products, but narratives that users can step into and make their own.

Safety in numbers

Each CRUMB is unique with personalized serial numbers. They are also connected through the CRUMB network. This allows for users to communicate/grow with eachother through CRUMB

My Work

My company exists to create technology that serves the whole human being—mind, body, and spirit. We specialize in developing tools and experiences that honor neurodivergent rhythms, emotional complexity, and the sacred nature of human connection.

Our flagship project, CRUMB, represents a new paradigm in human-computer interaction: a living ritual object that grows with its user, creating space for authentic expression and gentle presence in our increasingly digital world.

Every product we create is designed with intentionality, built to be a companion rather than just a tool, and crafted to remind users of the wonder inherent in existence.
